About

I'm a program manager and trainer based on the Eastside, and I've built a career around one core idea: technology and data should work for people, not the other way around.

For over 16 years — including a decade-long run at Liberty Mutual — I've designed learning ecosystems, built data infrastructure, and led programs that connect training activity to real business outcomes. Whether I'm architecting a SharePoint environment, building a Power BI dashboard for executives, or walking someone through a new tool for the first time, my job is making the complex feel obvious.

My background is in education (BS, Missouri State), and that foundation shapes everything I do. I think in terms of how people actually learn, not just what needs to be covered. I've layered that with a Project Management Certificate, a Microsoft Office Specialist certification, and a graduate certificate in internet marketing from USF.

Lately I find myself gravitating toward teams that want to go AI-first but haven't quite figured out the roadmap yet. That gap between ambition and execution is exactly where I do my best work.
